Book Nerds is a platform created by bookworms who recognised themselves not alone in the world, so they decided to create a community which would help them interract with others all around the world. It started as a simple small community but now it has over 8 million users who are registered as premiums but mostly freemium readers. Book nerds has a connection with the most recognised authors all over the world ; and they have created a way of helping writers access their books for free as long as they have access to internet and they even have a private accounts where users could pm them and give their suggestions and feedback. Book Nerds has been able to help some of the unrecognised writters reveal their hidden amazing talents. Where each user is offered a space to share their writing as long as they want to, it could be used as a private diary or it could be used as a draft for those who are still trying to unveil their talent. whatever it is used doesn't matter all that concerns Book Nerds community is that each user is able to feel at home every time they open they tabs to join the community which is always here waiting for new lovers of books.
